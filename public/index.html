Welcome to Memory Machine!

This app lets you turn your memories into a fun, retro claw machine game.

To get started:
• Click "Build a Machine" to enter your memories one by one — including a date, short story, and photo.
• Once you're done, you’ll receive a unique code you can share with friends or loved ones.

To play:
• Click "Play with a Machine" and enter a code shared with you.
• Use the claw to catch memory balls. Each ball reveals a surprise memory when opened.

Have fun reminiscing and sharing your special moments!
