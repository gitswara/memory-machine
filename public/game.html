Loading your claw machine!
How to Play:

• Press “Start” to begin moving the claw left and right.
• Press “Drop” to try and catch a memory ball.

If you catch one:
• The ball will move to the prize area.
• Click the ball to reveal a memory — including a story, date, and photo.

You have a limited number of tries based on how many memories were added.

When you’re done, you can press “Reset” to play again and revisit the memories.

Enjoy reliving your moments!
